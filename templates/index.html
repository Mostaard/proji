About us

Three years ago, a friend’s celebration holiday guided us east from Capetown and Stellenbosch, to Franschhoek. We were staying in a quaint holiday home run by fellow Belgians. They owned this little holiday cottage in the town centre, which had stolen our hearts immediately. Fortunately enough, we were able to purchase said cottage from them not much later. It is around this time that our little baby girl was born. We felt compelled to take her with us to Franschhoek when she was only 5 weeks old. It is her superstar namesake that rests upon the cottage, ‘Mona Luci’.

With all the legalities and paperwork that inevitably is connected to the rental market, it was only very recently that we were able to offer the cottage up for rent. This included our renovation plans and the possibility of expanding the accommodations in the years to come. We invested a lot of time in finding the right people to manage the premises and take care of our future guests. We want to guarantee our guests receive the best possible local hosts to ensure a dedicated personalized service. Having two resident ladies that have lived in Franschhoek almost their entire life and have years of experience in hosting guests, they are the living guidebook to Franschhoek and they will be happy and more than able to answer any question you might have.

Feel free to check our Instagram account, which is updated regularly with new photos of our experience in and around the West Cape, and in Villa Mona Luci in particular.
